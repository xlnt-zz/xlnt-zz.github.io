About

Jenny Neil, M. Ed., C. Psych.

I am a member of the College of Psychologists of Ontario with declared competency in Counselling and Forensic Psychology (Registrant # 6325).  As a Registered Psychologist, I currently operate a solo private practice in Kingston, ON. I obtained an Honours Undergraduate Degree in Psychology in 2005 and a Master’s of Counselling Psychology in 2009 from the University of Western Ontario. Since completion of graduate training, I have passionately worked with individuals for over 10 years in a variety of clinical settings within the Criminal Justice System with adults experiencing a broad range of issues including but not limited to stress, anxiety, depression, trauma, emotional instability, distress intolerance, relationship difficulties, abuse, addiction, neglect, grief, bereavement, and living with major mental illness. Throughout my career I have obtained specialized training in various modalities, with extensive experience and interest in both the assessment and treatment of trauma-related symptoms and PTSD.
