Individual Therapy

Individual therapy begins with an intake session, where you will be given an opportunity to provide background details related to yourself and the reasons you are seeking therapy. Next, therapeutic goals will be discussed for our work together. We decide these goals collaboratively and identify a realistic timeline to getting to where you want to be. Throughout the course of therapy, we periodically and collaboratively review progress toward your goals and adjust them as necessary.

Therapy sessions are 50 minutes in length. Sessions occur weekly, bi-weekly or monthly. The fee is $225 per session. My service is covered by most extended health plans. Given coverage varies among providers, please check directly with yours to see if you are covered for therapy with a Registered Psychologist belonging to the College of Psychologist of Ontario. I am also a registered provider with Veterans Affair and Blue Cross. I accept payment by credit card.

I work with adults (age 18+) on an individual basis.
